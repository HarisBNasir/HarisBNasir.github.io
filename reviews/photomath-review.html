Frequently Asked Questions

Is Photomath Plus worth the subscription cost?

+

For regular users, absolutely. The animated tutorials and detailed explanations provide significantly more educational value than the free version. Students using it several times per week will find it pays for itself in time saved and better understanding.

Can Photomath solve word problems?

+

Photomath has limited word problem capabilities compared to its equation solving. It works best with clearly written mathematical expressions rather than narrative-style problems. For word-heavy math, Wolfram Alpha or Mathway may be better options.

Does Photomath show work for all problems?

+

The free version shows basic steps for most problems, while Plus provides comprehensive step-by-step solutions with multiple methods for nearly all problems. Some extremely advanced or obscure problems may have limited explanations.

Can teachers detect if students use Photomath?

+

Teachers can often recognize when answers are copied without understanding, especially if students can't explain their work. Photomath is best used as a learning aid rather than a way to shortcut homework.

What math levels does Photomath cover?

+

Photomath covers arithmetic, algebra (I & II), geometry, trigonometry, calculus (introductory), and statistics. It's suitable from elementary school through early college, though advanced college math may require specialized tools.
