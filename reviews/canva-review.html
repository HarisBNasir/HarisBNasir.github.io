Frequently Asked Questions

Is Canva Pro worth it for freelancers?

+

Absolutely. At $12.99/month, it pays for itself with 1-2 projects by eliminating designer fees. The Brand Kit alone ensures professional consistency across all client materials.

How does Canva's AI compare to hiring a designer?

+

For most freelance needs (social graphics, presentations, basic branding), Canva AI produces professional results. Complex illustrations or high-end branding still require human designers.

Can I use Canva for client work commercially?

+

Yes! All Pro license assets can be used for client work. Just ensure you have the proper license for any premium photos used in final deliverables.

How accurate is the AI design generation?

+

The initial designs are about 70-80% there. You'll need to tweak colors, fonts, and layouts to perfectly match your brand, especially early on as the AI learns your style.

What's the best way to organize Canva for multiple clients?

+

Create a folder for each client, then subfolders by project type (social, presentations, etc.). Use the Brand Kit to save each client's colors/fonts for quick switching.
