Frequently Asked Questions

Is Notion AI worth the additional cost?

+

For power users who spend significant time in Notion, the AI add-on can be worthwhile for its writing assistance and summarization features. Casual users may find the free tier sufficient. Business teams often see the most value from the automation and knowledge management capabilities.

How does Notion AI handle privacy and data security?

+

Notion states that your data isn't used to train their AI models, and all processing happens within their secure infrastructure. However, as with any cloud service, sensitive information should be carefully considered before inclusion. Enterprise plans offer additional security controls.

Can I use Notion AI without the main Notion platform?

+

No, Notion AI is fully integrated into the Notion ecosystem and can't be used as a standalone product. If you're looking for a general-purpose AI writing tool, alternatives like ChatGPT or Claude might be more appropriate.

Does Notion AI work with imported documents or PDFs?

+

Yes, but with limitations. Notion AI can summarize and analyze text from imported documents, but the accuracy depends on the quality of text extraction. Scanned PDFs may not work well unless they've been OCR-processed.

Can Notion AI generate images or just text?

+

Currently, Notion AI is text-only. For AI image generation within Notion, you'd need to use embed integrations with tools like MidJourney or DALL-E, or paste images generated elsewhere.
