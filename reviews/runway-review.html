Frequently Asked Questions

Is Runway ML suitable for complete beginners?

+

While beginners can use Runway, it has a steeper learning curve than some consumer-focused tools. Those new to video editing may want to start with simpler apps before graduating to Runway's professional features.

How does the credit system work?

+

Credits are consumed based on tool usage and output length. Simple operations cost fewer credits than complex generations. Unused credits don't roll over, so plan your monthly usage accordingly.

Can I use Runway ML commercially?

+

Yes, all paid plans allow commercial use of generated content. Be aware that some AI models may have limitations regarding recognizable people or trademarks.

Does Runway offer any offline functionality?

+

No, all processing happens on Runway's servers. The desktop apps still require an internet connection. For offline work, consider traditional editing software with AI plugins.

How does Runway compare to MidJourney for video?

+

Runway offers more professional video editing tools and finer control over generations, while MidJourney focuses more on artistic exploration. Many professionals use both for different purposes.
