Frequently Asked Questions

Is Claude 3 Opus worth the Pro subscription?

+

For professionals working with long documents or needing high accuracy, absolutely. The Opus model's superior reasoning and massive context window justify the cost for researchers, analysts, and content professionals. Casual users may find the free Sonnet version sufficient.

How does Claude 3 compare to ChatGPT 4.5?

+

Claude excels at accuracy, long document analysis, and ethical considerations, while ChatGPT leads in creativity, coding, and general versatility. Claude's 200K token context dwarfs ChatGPT's 128K, making it better for lengthy materials.

Can Claude 3 access the internet?

+

No, Claude doesn't have built-in web browsing capabilities. It relies solely on its training data (current through late 2024) unless you provide recent information in your prompts or upload current documents.

What file types can Claude analyze?

+

Claude supports PDFs, Word docs, Excel spreadsheets, PowerPoint files, TXT files, and even images with text (though it can't interpret non-text visual elements). The Pro version handles larger files more reliably.

Why is Claude sometimes overly cautious?

+

Anthropic's Constitutional AI approach prioritizes safety, which can result in more conservative responses. This reduces harmful outputs but may limit creativity. You can often get more detailed responses by asking follow-up questions.
