Frequently Asked Questions

Is Grammarly Go worth the Premium subscription?

+

For professionals, students, or anyone who writes frequently, yes. The tone adjustments, style suggestions, and plagiarism checker provide significant value over the free version. Casual writers may find the free version sufficient.

How does Grammarly Go handle privacy?

+

Grammarly states they encrypt all user data and don't sell personal information. However, for highly sensitive documents, you may want to use the desktop app (which processes some data locally) rather than the browser extension.

Can Grammarly Go help with creative writing?

+

It's better for technical and business writing than creative works. While it can improve grammar and clarity, its style suggestions may conflict with artistic voice. Writers often pair it with specialized tools like ProWritingAid.

Does Grammarly Go work offline?

+

The desktop apps offer limited offline functionality, but full features require internet access. Mobile apps have the most limited offline capabilities.

How accurate is Grammarly Go's plagiarism checker?

+

It's good for detecting direct copying from online sources but shouldn't replace dedicated academic plagiarism tools. It checks against billions of web pages but has limited access to paywalled academic databases.
