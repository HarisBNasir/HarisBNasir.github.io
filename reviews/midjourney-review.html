Frequently Asked Questions

Is MidJourney suitable for commercial use?

+

Yes, but only on Standard and Pro plans. The Basic plan is for personal use only. MidJourney grants full commercial rights for images generated on paid plans, meaning you can sell, merchandise, and use them in commercial projects without additional licensing.

Why does MidJourney only work through Discord?

+

The Discord interface allows for rapid iteration, community learning, and scales more efficiently than traditional web interfaces. However, many users find this limiting, and MidJourney has hinted at developing a standalone interface in future updates.

How does MidJourney handle copyright and artist styles?

+

MidJourney v6 has implemented stronger ethical training protocols. While you can still request styles similar to famous artists, the system is less likely to directly replicate copyrighted characters or trademarked styles compared to earlier versions.

What's the difference between Fast and Relaxed modes?

+

Fast mode uses your monthly GPU time allocation but processes immediately. Relaxed mode queues your job and processes when resources are available but doesn't deduct from your allocation. Pro plan users get unlimited Relaxed generations.

Can I use MidJourney for NSFW content?

+

No, MidJourney maintains strict content policies against adult content. For NSFW generation, you would need to use locally-run alternatives like Stable Diffusion with appropriate models.
