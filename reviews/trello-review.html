Frequently Asked Questions

Is Trello's AI worth upgrading from the free version?

+

For freelancers managing 3+ ongoing clients, absolutely. The time predictions and auto-scheduling alone can save 2-3 hours weekly. The Standard plan at $5/month offers the best value for most solopreneurs.

How does Trello's AI handle client collaboration?

+

You can share specific boards with clients while keeping others private. The AI will generate client-friendly status updates and can even suggest optimal times to request feedback based on their past response patterns.

Can Trello replace my time tracking tool?

+

Not fully. While it offers time estimates, you'll need Power-Ups like Harvest or Toggl Track for proper time tracking. The AI can however analyze your time estimates vs actuals to improve future predictions.

How secure is Trello for confidential client work?

+

Trello offers enterprise-grade security on paid plans. For highly sensitive projects, use private boards, enable two-factor authentication, and consider the Premium plan's advanced permissions.

What's the best way to organize Trello as a freelancer?

+

We recommend the "P.A.R.A" method: Projects (client work), Areas (skills/services), Resources (templates/tools), Archive (completed work). Create a master board with these four lists as your foundation.
