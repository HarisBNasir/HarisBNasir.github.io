Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use both Notion and Evernote together?

+

Yes, many users employ both tools for different purposes. A common workflow is using Evernote for quick capture and web clipping, then organizing selected content in Notion for long-term management and collaboration.

Which app has better AI capabilities?

+

Notion AI offers more comprehensive generation capabilities (full documents, databases), while Evernote's AI excels at enhancing existing notes (formatting, search). For pure note-taking enhancement, Evernote's AI is more refined, while Notion offers broader AI-powered workspace features.

Is Notion too complicated for personal use?

+

Notion can be overwhelming initially for personal use, but many users find its flexibility worthwhile after the learning curve. Start with simple notes and gradually explore databases and templates. Evernote remains simpler for basic note-taking needs.

Which app handles PDFs better?

+

Evernote currently has superior PDF handling, especially for annotation and search within PDFs. Notion can store and display PDFs but lacks advanced PDF-specific features.

How do the mobile apps compare?

+

Evernote's mobile app is faster and more reliable for quick capture. Notion's mobile app has improved significantly but can still be sluggish with complex workspaces. For pure mobile note-taking, Evernote has the edge.

Both Notion AI and Evernote offer free plans so you can try them before committing.
