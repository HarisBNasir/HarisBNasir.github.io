Frequently Asked Questions

Is Notion AI worth the additional cost?

+

For power users, yes. The AI features can save hours per week on content generation, summarization, and task management. Try the free trial to evaluate.

How does Notion AI compare to ChatGPT?

+

Notion AI is optimized for productivity within Notion's environment, while ChatGPT is more general purpose. Notion AI understands your content context better.

Can Notion AI access all my private data?

+

No, Notion AI only processes content when you explicitly invoke it, and doesn't use your data to train public models according to their privacy policy.

What's the best way to learn Notion databases?

+

Start with simple lists, then gradually explore properties, views, and relations. Use AI to explain concepts as you go with "/explain databases".
