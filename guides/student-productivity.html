Automated Workflows

Connect your tools for seamless productivity:

Research Paper Workflow

  1. Elicit finds relevant papers → Saves to Zotero
  2. Zotero generates citations → Exports to Notion
  3. Scholarcy creates summaries → Imports to Anki
  4. Notion AI helps draft outline → Jasper expands sections
  5. Grammarly checks final draft
