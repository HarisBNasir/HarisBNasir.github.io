Frequently Asked Questions

How much does business automation with AI cost?

+

Costs vary from $20/month for basic tools to $50,000+ for enterprise RPA solutions. Most SMBs can start with $100-300/month tools.

What's the easiest process to automate first?

+

Start with data transfer between apps (e.g., form submissions to CRM) or automated email responses.

How do we ensure data security with automation?

+

Choose tools with SOC 2 compliance, implement role-based access, and avoid automating processes with sensitive data initially.

Will automation eliminate jobs?

+

Typically no - it reallocates human effort to higher-value work. Most businesses reassign rather than reduce staff.
